ABOUT

ON-VESH

ON-VESH is an abbreviation for ONLINE ANVESH. It is an Application and Website designed for the students of VIVEKANAND EDUCATION SOCIETY POLYTECHNIC so that they can book tickets and register for the Events in their college fest(ANVESH). It is easy and relaible and each and ever student can book his / her tickets within few minutes.
